Design Section 2: Game Progression & High Score

Session-based scoring and reward system

HUD Layout
SCORE: 01420 BEST: 03500
TRACK_LEVEL: 1 [RESUME_FACT_LOCKED]
Session Logic
  • Storage: sessionStorage (cleared on tab close).
  • Persistence: Updates in real-time as the user plays.
  • UI: High score appears in the game's top-right corner.

Progression Mechanics

  • Scoring: Points awarded for every second survived and for "grazing" obstacles (extra risk = extra points).
  • Level Triggers: Every 500 points, a "Data Fact" about you is revealed (e.g., "Akash once automated a 4-hour task into 5 minutes!").
  • Game Over: Simple "SYSTEM_CRASH" message with a button to REBOOT (Retry).
